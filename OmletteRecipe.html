Veggie Omelette
11:32 am

Ready In: 15 minutes - Serves 1

Ingredients

1. 3 eggs, beaten

2. 1/4 cup mushroom, sliced

3. 1/4 cup green pepper, diced

4. 3 tablespoons green onions, sliced

5. 1/4 cup cup cheddar cheese (optional)

Directions

1. Heat frying pan, spray with nonstick spray or coat the bottom of the pan with oil, or butter.

2. Mix together beaten eggs, mushrooms, green peppers and green onions.

3. When pan is hot enough add egg mixture and cook until almost set, until you can flip it over, cook on the other side until set. If desired, top omelette with cheese and fold over.

4. Serve alone or with fresh fruit. Enjoy!
